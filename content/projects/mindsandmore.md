**minds&more** is a Belgian consultancy that provides "matching minds" in marketing, sales, HR, and finance — advising on strategy and aligning consultants with clients. Its site needed to feel as sharp as its positioning.

## The challenge

The substance was there; the experience needed lifting. The goal was a more refined, modern front-end — better interactions and motion — without rebuilding the site from scratch.

## What we built

Delivered at Cherry Pulp:

- An upgraded **UI/UX** layer over the existing site
- Refined interactions and **motion effects** bringing the front-end to life

## My role

I handled the UI/UX upgrade — implementing the refreshed front-end and the **motion and animation effects** that give the site its polish.

## Outcome

minds&more got a noticeably more polished, modern feel — same substance, sharper experience. Live at [mindsandmore.biz](https://www.mindsandmore.biz).
