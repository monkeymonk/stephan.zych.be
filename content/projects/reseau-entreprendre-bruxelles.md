**Réseau Entreprendre Bruxelles** is a Brussels network that supports entrepreneurs. The brief was a landing page built to convince entrepreneurs to join as members — presenting the network's values and advantages, and putting them in touch.

<video src="/assets/content/reseau-entreprendre-bruxelles.mp4" controls playsinline preload="metadata" poster="/assets/content/reseau-entreprendre-bruxelles-still.webp"></video>

## The challenge

A membership pitch in a single page: communicate the network's values and benefits in a lively, welcoming way, and turn interest into contact — all on a foundation reliable enough to run and update without friction.

## What we built

Delivered at Cherry Pulp:

- A dynamic, conversion-focused landing page presenting the network's advantages and values
- A contact and membership-enquiry flow
- A **WordPress** integration that lets the team run and update the site themselves

![Réseau Entreprendre Bruxelles — the network's brand identity](/assets/content/reseau-entreprendre-bruxelles-brand.webp)

## My role

I worked on the **frontend**, and on the **WordPress** integration and setup — building the landing page's front end, wiring it into WordPress, and getting the site stood up and running.

## The interesting part

A conversion-focused landing page has to feel lively and hand-crafted, yet still live inside **WordPress** so the team can keep editing it. The interesting part was the **integration**: translating a dynamic, single-page design into WordPress cleanly — and setting the site up — so it stayed both persuasive to visitors and maintainable for the network, rather than a bespoke page nobody could touch.

## Outcome

The network got a friendly, persuasive front door for recruiting members — a conversion-focused landing page, integrated into WordPress so the team could keep it current.
