**Boxify** is a storage service with a simple promise: "you box it, we store it." We built it an intuitive website and app where customers manage the goods they've put into storage, backed by an admin interface and an **API**.

## The challenge

Storage is a service people want to forget about — which means the software has to be effortless. Customers needed to size what they wanted to store, then see and manage their items without thinking, while the team needed real tools to administer it all behind the scenes.

## What we built

Delivered at Cherry Pulp:

- A **storage calculator** that sizes the space — and the price — a customer actually needs
- An intuitive **customer portal** and app for managing stored goods
- An **admin interface** for running the service
- An **API** tying the platform, app, and admin together

## My role

I built the **whole SaaS frontend** — the customer-facing UI and UX. The most interesting parts were the **storage calculator** and the interface customers use to **actually manage their storage**: turning a logistics workflow into something clean and obvious to use.

## Outcome

Boxify customers got a portal they can size, fill, and manage their storage from, and the team got the admin tooling and API to run the service behind it. Live at [boxify.be](https://www.boxify.be).
